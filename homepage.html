Welcome to Study Corner!

Study Corner is your ultimate online study companion. With a range of helpful tools and resources, we aim to enhance your productivity, organization, and academic success. Whether you need to manage your time effectively with the Pomodoro Timer, stay on top of your tasks with the Todo List, or calculate your CGPA effortlessly, Study Corner has got you covered.

Our Pomodoro Timer helps you optimize your study sessions by breaking them into focused intervals and rest breaks. Stay motivated and maintain your concentration throughout your study sessions with this proven technique.

The Todo List feature allows you to organize your tasks and prioritize your workload. Keep track of your assignments, projects, and deadlines, ensuring that nothing falls through the cracks.

Calculating your CGPA is made simple with our CGPA Calculator. Just enter your grades and credits, and let Study Corner handle the calculations for you. Stay informed about your academic progress and plan your future semesters effectively.

Study Corner is here to support you on your educational journey. Join us today and make the most out of your study time!
